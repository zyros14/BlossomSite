About Blossom

A survival world that feels relaxed, not rushed.

Blossom SMP is built for players who want a simple, friendly Minecraft survival experience without pay-to-win features or overcomplicated menus.

You can build at your own pace, claim your land, travel with Waystones, chat with nearby players using proximity voice, and enjoy a server that keeps survival feeling like survival.

Features

Everything you need, nothing unfair.

Blossom adds useful systems that make survival smoother, while keeping the main game fair for everyone.
