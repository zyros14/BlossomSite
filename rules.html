Server Rules

Short, fair, and enforced consistently. Breaking these gets you muted, jailed, or banned depending on severity.

00
Welcome

Blossom SMP Rules

Welcome to Blossom SMP! These rules exist to keep the server fair, chill, and enjoyable for everyone. Ignorance is not an excuse.

01
Community

Respect Everyone

No harassment, bullying, hate speech, or targeted insults. No racism, sexism, homophobia, or slurs (even “jokingly”). Don’t start drama or intentionally provoke others.

02
Chat

No Spam or Disruption

No message spam, emoji spam, or repeated characters. No mass pinging users or staff. Don’t flood chat with commands or pointless messages. Voice chat spam (soundboards, screaming, etc.) is not allowed.

03
Fair play

No Cheating or Exploits

No hacked clients, x-ray, reach, fly, or PvP cheats. No duplication glitches or abusing bugs. If you find a bug, report it immediately. Macros or autoclickers that give PvP advantage are not allowed.

04
Building

No Griefing or Theft

Do not break, steal, or modify other players’ builds. No raiding bases unless explicitly allowed by server rules. Do not empty chests, farms, or storage without permission.

05
Content

Chat & Content Rules

Keep chat PG-13 (no explicit sexual content). No NSFW, disturbing, or graphic content. Avoid excessive toxicity or insulting humor. English is preferred in public chat for moderation.

06
Promotion

Advertising & Links

No server ads, Discord invites, or self-promotion. No suspicious or unsafe links. You may share content only in designated channels if allowed.

07
Staff

Staff Decisions

Staff instructions must be followed in-game and in Discord. Do not argue during punishment situations. If you have an issue, use tickets instead.

08
Integrity

Server Integrity

Do not impersonate staff or other players. Do not abuse alt accounts to bypass punishments. Do not intentionally lag the server or cause crashes.

09
Punishments

Enforcement

Punishments may include warnings, mutes, kicks, rollbacks, or bans depending on severity.

Questions about a rule?

Ask in the Discord — staff are around most of the day.
